Here is the home of the machine learning group in department of astronomy, Tsinghua university. We have interests in applying machine learning to the research of astronomy.


In this small group of family, we encourage members to:
  • give an introduction of your own work, no matter whether they are submitted or not;
  • give a practice talk before giving an informal one in the conferences;
  • share with us the interesting ML papers on daily arXiv;
  • share some specific ML topics/knowledge your have learnt;
  • give an overview of some subfields or literature review ;
  • share with us the key takeaway of any related (Virtual) conferences you have attended;
